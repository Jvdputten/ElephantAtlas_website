THE BAND

Elephant Atlas are a 2-piece Dutch Rock band from Veldhoven. The band was only recently founded in 2020, however, the duo has been playing together for the last 15 years which is apparent from their synergy. Their unique brand of entertainment fuses punchy Punk-Pop riffs with elements from blues, metal, country, and even hiphop. Hailed as the 'best thing since sliced bread' by everyone and their grandma, Elephant Atlas is bound to put up a good show.
